# Connect J.A.R.V.I.S. to Your Mobile Phone to Use Anywhere

This guide outlines how to connect your mobile device to your local J.A.R.V.I.S. server using a secure, free network mesh, and how to bypass mobile browser security restrictions so your microphone and voice features function properly over a local connection.

================================================================================
PART 1: CONNECT YOUR PHONE USING TAILSCALE (FREE VPN)
================================================================================

1. Install Tailscale on your PC:
   - Download and install Tailscale for Windows from https://tailscale.com/download
   - Launch it and log in (you can use your Google or GitHub account).

2. Install Tailscale on your Phone:
   - Download "Tailscale" from the Apple App Store or Google Play Store.
   - Open the app and log in using the exact same account you used on your PC.
   - Toggle the VPN switch to "Connected".

3. Find your PC's Magic IP Address:
   - Open the Tailscale app on your phone.
   - Look for your PC in the device list and copy the IP address (it will look like 100.x.x.x).
   - Your final URL to access Jarvis will look like this: http://YOUR_TAILSCALE_IP:PORT 
     (Example: http://100.123.45.67:3000)


================================================================================
PART 2: UNLOCK BROWSER SECURITY FOR AUDIO & MIC ACCESS
================================================================================

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
METHOD A: FOR ANDROID (GOOGLE CHROME)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
1. Open Google Chrome on your Android phone.
2. In the URL address bar, type exactly this and hit enter:
   chrome://flags

3. In the search box at the top of the page, type:
   unsafely-treat-insecure-origin-as-secure

4. Change the setting from "Disabled" to "Enabled".
5. In the text box right below it, paste your full Tailscale URL with the port:
   http://YOUR_TAILSCALE_IP:PORT

6. Tap the "Relaunch" button at the bottom of the screen to restart Chrome.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
METHOD B: FOR IPHONE (SAFARI)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
1. Open the "Settings" app on your iPhone.
2. Scroll down and tap on "Safari".
3. Scroll all the way to the bottom and tap on "Advanced".
4. Make sure the toggle for "Web Inspector" is turned ON.
5. Make sure "Local Network Access" is allowed for Safari if prompted.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
METHOD C: FOR IPHONE (GOOGLE CHROME)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
1. Open Google Chrome on your iPhone.
2. In the URL address bar, type exactly this and hit enter:
   chrome://flags

3. In the search box, type:
   unsafely-treat-insecure-origin-as-secure

4. Change the setting to "Enabled".
5. Paste your full Tailscale URL with the port into the text box:
   http://YOUR_TAILSCALE_IP:PORT

6. Relaunch the Chrome app.


================================================================================
PART 3: ACCESSING J.A.R.V.I.S.
================================================================================

1. Make sure Tailscale is connected/turned ON on both your PC and your phone.
2. Make sure your local Jarvis application server is running on your PC.
3. Open your mobile browser, type in your full URL link (e.g., http://100.x.x.x:3000), and hit enter! Everything will load with full mic and audio privileges unlocked.
